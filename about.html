About Me

I am Dr. Balasaheb Patekar, a dedicated veterinary professional with a passion for animal care. My journey began with completing my 10th standard, followed by 11th and 12th in Science. I pursued a Diploma in Pharmacy and later specialized in veterinary medicine with a 2-year Veterinary Diploma. With 1.5 years of hands-on experience in this field, I have gained expertise in diagnosing and treating various animal health conditions. My mission is to provide compassionate care and the best treatment for pets and livestock.
